Privacy Policy


We do our best to protect your data. We only store personal data if it is necessary for using our website "tipfield.com".

When visiting "tipfield.com", the following data available from your browser is recorded for logging purposes by our webserver:

  • web request
  • IP address
  • browser type
  • browser language
  • date and time

Further we store session cookies in your browser that can only be accessed by our website. Using them, we can memorise if you are already logged in and in which language you want to use "tipfield.com".

After logging in with Facebook, the following data gets transmitted to us (only with your consent):

  • Permissions Reference - Basic Information (only the part about "Basic Information" is relevant)

They are used for assigning your tips to you and how you compare against your friends.

Upon request, we provide you immediately and free of charge with all information that we store about you. This information can be provided to you electronically.

For any further questions please feel free to contact us under tipfield@dimond.de.
